Forensic Data & Discovery Requirements

To provide a definitive Merit Review, our firm analyzes "native" digital data often omitted from standard medical records. We assist Counsel in requesting the following technical assets:

Cardiac Monitor Waveforms

Full "Code Summary" logs from LifePak/Zoll devices, including continuous ECG and EtCO2 waveforms to verify clinical interventions.

Ventilator Data Analytics

Forensic audit of digital event logs and breath-by-breath mechanics from transport ventilators (e.g., Hamilton, Revel, LTV, or Zoll series).

CAD & AVL Telematics

Millisecond-accurate dispatch logs and GPS telematics for objective timeline reconstruction.

Agency-Specific Protocols

Audit of local clinical guidelines against the established Wisconsin State Standard of Care.
